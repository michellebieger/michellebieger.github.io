Software development

This is a page dedicated to the work I've accomplished in software development. Some of my software development is more directly related to research, and so can be found on that page.

Senior software engineer at digiLab

digiLab is a deep-tech start-up specialising in tackling sustainable engineering problems with machine learning.

Developing the twinLab product

One of the flagship products of digiLab is our twinLab software, which works using our proprietary machine learning library. Our software can help engineers to make predictions and quantify the uncertainty on their models.
As a senior software engineer, I help to build twinLab across the full stack: from library, to API, to customer interfaces. For more details, please get in contact for my CV.
During my time at digiLab, I also had the opportunity to work on our Academy product, creating a lecture series from the ground-up on AI ethics. Check out AI Ethics: Fairness and Balance in Modern Machine Learning.

SCRUM master and product management work

Since December 2023, I am SCRUM master for the product team, helping to allocate work amongst our graduate team. I also help to organise and develop the backlog in a manner that aligns with the expectations of our sales and tech teams.
